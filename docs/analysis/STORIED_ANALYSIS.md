## 1. Overview

Storied (formerly World Archives) is a modern family history platform that emphasizes **storytelling** over traditional genealogy. Launched with a rebrand in January 2023, it positions itself as "the next chapter in family history" and uses AI-powered tools for story creation.

**Key Stats:**
- Billions of historical records
- Newspaper archive dating back to 1607
- AI-powered storytelling tools
- StoriedBooks physical book printing
- BBB Accredited (A rating)

**Positioning:** "Stories are what matter" - Most closely aligned with Gene-Tree's philosophy

## 10. Ideas for Gene-Tree

### Key Insights
Storied is the **most philosophically aligned** competitor - both believe stories matter more than just names and dates. This makes them a direct competitor in positioning.

### Differentiation Opportunities
1. **Free tier** - Storied has none, Gene-Tree offers 50 profiles free
2. **Verification system** - Gene-Tree's unique advantage
3. **Granular privacy** - Per-field vs group-level
4. **Cultural awareness** - Multi-language (EN/RU) support
5. **Living family focus** - Connections, not just stories

### High Priority (Implement)
1. **Story prompts** - Guided questions for memory capture
2. **Audio stories** - Voice recording capability
3. **Annual pricing option** - $19.99-99.99/yr is very competitive
4. **AI writing assistance** - Story enhancement tools

### Medium Priority (Consider)
5. **Photo/video stories** - Rich media integration
6. **Family groups** - Private sharing spaces
7. **Book printing** - Partner with printing service
8. **Resource center** - Educational content

### Low Priority (Monitor)
9. **Newspaper integration** - Partner vs build
10. **Historical records** - Focus on living family first
11. **Podcast/YouTube** - Content marketing

### What NOT to Copy
- **No free tier** - Gene-Tree should maintain free option
- **Auto-renewal only** - Offer flexibility
- **Records-first** - Keep stories and relationships as primary

---

## Summary

Storied is Gene-Tree's **most direct competitor** in philosophy. Both believe stories and memories matter more than traditional genealogy. However, Storied still leans heavily on historical records, while Gene-Tree focuses on living family connections and verification.

**Key Takeaway:** Gene-Tree should differentiate through:
1. **Free tier** - Storied's weakness
2. **Verification-based trust** - Unique feature
3. **Granular privacy** - Stronger than group-level
4. **Living family focus** - Not just historical stories
5. **Competitive annual pricing** - Consider $79.99/yr option

Storied's AI storytelling tools and audio recording features are worth studying for Gene-Tree's story preservation capabilities.
